Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

+

We're asking for formal attire. Think elegant dresses, suits, and kilts are absolutely encouraged! The ceremony and reception will be indoors, but we'll have some time in the gardens for photos.

Can I bring a plus-one?

+

Due to venue capacity, we're only able to accommodate guests formally invited on your invitation. If you received a plus-one, it will be indicated on your invitation.

Are children welcome?

+

We love your little ones, but we've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a chance to enjoy a night off!

What about dietary requirements?

+

Please let us know about any dietary requirements or allergies when you RSVP, and we'll make sure you're well catered for on the day.

Will there be transportation?

+

We'll have shuttle buses running between the recommended hotels and Taypark House. Details will be sent closer to the date.

What about gifts?

+

Your presence is the greatest gift! However, if you'd like to give something, we'd be grateful for contributions to our honeymoon fund. Details will be included with your invitation.
